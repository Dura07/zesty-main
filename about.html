About Us

About Us

Welcome to Zest Lounge and Bar, where sophistication, style, and vibrant energy come together. More than just a bar, Zest offers an upscale experience with handcrafted cocktails, gourmet dishes, and premium bottle service in an elegant setting. Unwind in our stylish lounges, celebrate special moments, or dance the night away to live DJs and curated playlists. With world-class service, exclusive VIP seating, and an electric atmosphere, Zest Lounge and Bar is the ultimate destination for unforgettable nights.

Our Mission

Savor our expertly crafted selection of small plates and appetizers, each designed to perfectly pair with your favorite drink and enhance your experience. From rich, savory bites to indulgent delights, our menu caters to every craving with bold flavors and exquisite presentation. As the night comes alive, let the pulse of the music move you. With live DJs spinning an eclectic mix of beats across genres and eras, every rhythm invites you to dance, celebrate, and create unforgettable moments.
